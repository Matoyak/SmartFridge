Worldwide

The amount of food lost and wasted every year is equal to more than half of the world’s annual cereals crops (2.3 billion tons in 2009)

USA

In the USA, 30-40% of the food supply is wasted, equaling more than 20 pounds of food per person per month.

What you can do?

Wasting food is often a subconscious act - become aware of how much food you throw away. Save your food, save your money and save the environment.
